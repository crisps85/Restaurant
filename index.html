Happy House in Grays is a Chinese restaurant offering some of the very best meat, fish and vegetable dishes. We have become famous for offering the freshest Chinese dishes, simply cooked; we believe that the finest products speak for themsleves. The restaurant is on Southend Road and offers a nice stroll through Grays. A full a la carte menu is available from Monday-Sunday.
